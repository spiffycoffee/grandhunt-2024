What is a metapuzzle?

A metapuzzle is a puzzle that uses answers from the previous puzzles in the round, but you'll need to figure out how to use these answers to extract a final answer. You do not need to solve all puzzles in the round in order to solve a metapuzzle, though usually having more puzzles solved (and thus more answers) will make it easier to solve a meta.

How hard are these puzzles?

These puzzles are built to be solved by brand new puzzlers. The difficulty will increase as you solve more puzzles, so we recommend new puzzlers work in teams, but experienced puzzlers can attempt this hunt solo. We're aiming for a difficulty around the recent Paradox Puzzlehunt or Hunt 20 Puzzle Hunt. Hopefully this hunt will serve as a cooldown to the 2023 MIT Mystery Hunt, as well as a fun introduction to online puzzle hunts!!
