This puzzle utilizes the NATO phonetic alphabet.

To solve this puzzle, the solver needed to search the military document and pick out (in order) the twelve hidden NATO keywords. In order, the words were:


LIMA, INDIA, BRAVO, ROMEO, ALPHA, ROMEO, YANKEE, BRAVO, OSCAR, OSCAR, KILO, SIERRA.


With the NATO phonetic alphabet, each of these code words stands for one letter. When translated, you get the answer: LIBRARYBOOKS
