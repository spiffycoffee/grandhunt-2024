There are two main steps that need to be completed to solve the Spell Chek puzzle.


Step 1: Solvers may notice a number of spelling errors in the provided checklist. The name of

the puzzle “Spell Chek” also hints to the solver that they need to spell check the document.

Each line on the checklist has one missing letter. Finding the missing letter for each line and

reading those letters from top to bottom reveals the secret message:“USE MORSE CODE BULLETS”.


Step 2: As indicated by the message hidden in the typos, the bullet points of the checklist are

actually Morse Code. They are composed of dots and dashes, with the check marks indicating the

start of a new letter. Translating the morse code gives the solver their final answer.


ANSWER IS CARKEYS.
